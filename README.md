# 🛍️ Customer Shopping Behavior Analysis using Python, SQL & Power BI

## 📊 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing customer shopping behavior by using an end-to-end data analytics process with **Python, SQL, and Power BI**. The main goal of this project is to understand how customers make purchasing decisions and identify patterns that can help improve business performance.

The analysis explores customer preferences, product performance, subscription behavior, and purchase trends. By combining data cleaning, database analysis, and dashboard visualization, this project provides meaningful insights that can support better business decisions.

Through this project, important business questions such as customer engagement, revenue contribution, product popularity, and customer loyalty were analyzed.

---

## 📂 Dataset

The dataset contains customer shopping information collected from retail transactions. It includes customer details, purchase records, and behavioral information.

### Dataset includes:

### Customer Information
- Customer ID  
- Age  
- Gender  
- Location  

### Shopping Details
- Product Purchased  
- Category  
- Purchase Amount  
- Payment Method  
- Shipping Type  

### Customer Behavior Data
- Subscription Status  
- Previous Purchases  
- Review Rating  
- Discount Applied  
- Promo Code Used  

### Time-related Data
- Season  
- Purchase Frequency  

This dataset helps in understanding customer buying patterns and identifying factors that influence purchasing behavior.

## 📐 DAX Measures Used in the Project

To build the Power BI dashboard, several DAX measures were created to calculate important business metrics.

### Number of Customers
Used to calculate the total number of customers.

```DAX
Number of Customers =
COUNT('public customer'[customer_id])
```

### Average Purchase Amount
Used to calculate the average amount spent by customers.

```DAX
Average Purchase Amount =
AVERAGE('public customer'[purchase_amount])
```

### Average Review Rating
Used to calculate the average customer review rating.

```DAX
Average Review Rating =
AVERAGE('public customer'[review_rating])
```

These measures help in understanding customer volume, spending behavior, and satisfaction levels.

## 📊 Dashboard Visualizations

An interactive dashboard was created in Power BI to present insights in a visual and easy-to-understand format.

The dashboard includes the following visualizations:

### KPI Cards
Displays:
- Total number of customers  
- Average purchase amount  
- Average review rating  

These cards provide a quick overview of business performance.

### Subscription Status Analysis
A donut chart showing the percentage of subscribed and non-subscribed customers.

This helps in understanding customer engagement and loyalty.

### Revenue by Category
A bar chart showing revenue generated by each product category.

This helps identify the most profitable product categories.

### Sales by Category
Displays the total number of sales across different categories.

This helps compare product demand.

### Revenue by Age Group
Shows how much revenue each age group contributes.

This helps identify high-value customer segments.

### Sales by Age Group
Shows purchase activity across different age groups.

This helps understand customer buying behavior by age.

### Filters / Slicers
Interactive filters for:
- Subscription Status  
- Gender  
- Category  
- Shipping Type  

These filters allow dynamic dashboard analysis.

## 📈 Business Insights

From the analysis, several important business insights were identified:

- Customers with subscriptions contribute a significant portion of revenue.
- Certain product categories generate higher revenue than others.
- Young adults and adults form the major customer base.
- Customer ratings provide valuable feedback about product quality.
- Repeat customers play an important role in overall business growth.
- Discounts and promotions influence customer purchase decisions.
- Customer preferences vary across product categories and payment methods.

These insights help the business understand customer behavior and improve decision-making.

## 💡 Business Recommendations

Based on the analysis, the following recommendations can help improve business performance:

- Strengthen subscription programs to increase customer loyalty.
- Focus marketing efforts on high-performing product categories.
- Improve customer experience based on product reviews and ratings.
- Use discounts strategically to increase sales while maintaining profit.
- Target high-value age groups through personalized marketing campaigns.
- Improve retention strategies for repeat customers.

By using these insights, the company can improve customer engagement, increase revenue, and build stronger customer relationships.
